# CIVITAS — Presentation Day Checklist

Use this checklist on the day of the demonstration to ensure all hardware, software, and backup materials are ready.

---

## 1. Morning of the Demo
- [ ] Close all heavy applications (Docker, IDEs, databases) on the presentation laptop to maximize free RAM.
- [ ] Set system brightness to 100% and connect the laptop to a power source.
- [ ] Connect to the venue WiFi and verify speed. Enable mobile hotspot on your phone as a backup connection.
- [ ] Turn on **Do Not Disturb** or **Focus Mode** on the presentation laptop to suppress email, Slack, and message notifications.
- [ ] Bookmark the local demo URL (`http://localhost:3000/`) and the deployed URL (`https://civitas-demo.web.app/`).
- [ ] Copy `demo_backup.mp4` to the desktop and a backup USB drive.
- [ ] Print the [docs/DEMO_SCRIPT_FINAL.md](file:///e:/civitas/docs/DEMO_SCRIPT_FINAL.md) presentation guide.

---

## 2. One Minute Before Pitch
- [ ] Load the demo URL in Chrome. Do not wait until you stand in front of judges to load the page.
- [ ] Clear browser cache (`Ctrl+Shift+R` or empty cache hard reload).
- [ ] Open the browser console to check for any critical JS startup warnings.
- [ ] Mute system audio (speakers) to prevent any audio feedback.
- [ ] Position the screen at a comfortable viewing angle for the judges.

---

## 3. During the Live Presentation
- [ ] Speak clearly and at a deliberate pace.
- [ ] Point at specific dashboard elements (Thought Stream terminal, comparisons, map) as you describe them.
- [ ] Keep an eye on the judges' expressions. If they look confused, slow down and explain the agent trade-offs.
- [ ] Let the pipeline run naturally; do not double-click or spam buttons.
- [ ] **Pivot Rule**: If the system is unresponsive for more than 15 seconds, launch `demo_backup.mp4` immediately.

---

## 4. Q&A Phase
- [ ] Open the Q&A sheet ([docs/JUDGE_TALKING_POINTS.md](file:///e:/civitas/docs/JUDGE_TALKING_POINTS.md)) on a secondary tablet or phone.
- [ ] Answer questions confidently and keep explanations limited to 1–2 concise sentences.
- [ ] If you do not know the answer, say: *"Excellent point, we have mapped that out in our future engineering roadmap and will verify the exact bounds."* (Never guess).
