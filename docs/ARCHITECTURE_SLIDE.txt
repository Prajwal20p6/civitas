[SLIDE TITLE: CIVITAS: Agentic Traffic Coordination]

1. PROBLEM STATEMENT:
   "Traditional sirens and emergency overrides are localized and blind to urban 
   congestion, causing delayed ambulances and triggering cascading city gridlock."

2. SOLUTION OVERVIEW:
   "An AI-native autonomous emergency traffic coordinator that negotiates 
   signal preemption corridors in real-time, resolving conflicts between 
   response speed and civilian fairness."

3. KEY DIFFERENTIATOR:
   "Multi-Agent Negotiation: Instead of traditional heuristic rules, we model 
   competing routing priorities (Speed-First vs. Fairness-First) as autonomous 
   agents. They negotiate through real-time traffic simulations, validated by 
   a human-in-the-loop approval gate."

4. TECH STACK:
   - Google Agent Development Kit (ADK) 2.0
   - Google Vertex AI (Gemini 2.5 Flash / Gemini 2.5 Pro)
   - FastAPI Backend & WebSockets
   - React Operator Dashboard with Google Maps JS SDK
   - Firebase Firestore & Realtime Database

5. RESULTS:
   - 90-second end-to-end interactive demo.
   - Deterministic scoring resolver guarantees predictable, safe outcomes.
   - Saves up to 60% of baseline transit times with minimal civilian traffic impact.

6. CALL TO ACTION:
   "Let's build safer, smarter cities that save lives in real-time. Join the 
   CIVITAS pilot today."
